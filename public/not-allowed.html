Types of Grievances NOT Allowed

Please avoid submitting grievances that fall under the following categories:

  • Duplicate Grievances: Repeating the same complaint multiple times is not allowed.
  • Irrelevant Grievances: Issues unrelated to academics, campus facilities, or student welfare will not be entertained.
  • Anonymous Complaints: Grievances without a valid name and contact details will not be considered.
  • Personal Disputes: Conflicts between students that are not related to academic or campus policies should be resolved personally.
  • False Accusations: Misleading or fabricated complaints against faculty, staff, or students are strictly prohibited.
  • Abusive or Offensive Language: Grievances containing disrespectful, vulgar, or offensive language will be rejected.
  • Grievances Without Evidence: Complaints must be backed with valid proof or supporting details for proper resolution.
  • Issues Already Addressed: If a grievance has already been resolved or answered, resubmission is unnecessary.
  • Hostel Roommate Issues: Personal roommate disputes should be handled with hostel authorities first.
  • Non-Educational Matters: Complaints about personal lifestyle choices, friendships, or social life are outside the scope of this portal.
